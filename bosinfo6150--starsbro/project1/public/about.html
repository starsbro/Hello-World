Cats, also called domestic cats (Felis catus), are small, carnivorous mammals. Cats have been domesticated (tamed) for nearly 10,000 years.They are one of the most popular pets in the world. Humans keep them for hunting mice and rats, and as friends. There are also farm cats, which keep mice and rats away; and feral cats, which are domestic cats that live away from humans.
